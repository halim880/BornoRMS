Admin User Manual

Guide to running the BornoBit Restaurant staff console (port 5002). Covers daily operations, inventory, finance, and administration. Sign in at /login with your staff account.

Roles. Access to each feature depends on your role: SuperAdminAdmin ManagerWaiter ChefCashier. Admin/SuperAdmin reach everything; other roles see only their permitted modules.

Operations

Orders →

View and manage incoming customer orders; print receipts.

POS →

Cash-register billing: build orders, discount, take payment.

Kitchen Display →

Live kitchen tickets, station view, sound alerts.

Operations Dashboard →

Live sales, open orders, table and kitchen state.

Cash Counter →

Count drawer cash and reconcile with transactions.

Categories, products, dining tables, QR codes.

Raw materials: receipts, movements, wastage, suppliers.
