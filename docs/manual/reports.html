Reports

/operations/reports/sales · /operations/reports/top-selling-items · /operations/reports/collection

Who can access: ManagerAdminSuperAdmin

Purpose

Business insight reports for sales performance, best sellers, and cash collection, plus a printable stock valuation.

Available reports

  • Sales (/operations/reports/sales) — revenue and order count by day/period.
  • Top Selling Items (/operations/reports/top-selling-items) — best-seller ranking.
  • Collection (/operations/reports/collection) — cash collected by method/date.
  • Stock Valuation PDF (/reports/stock/valuation.pdf) — downloadable inventory value.

How to run

  1. Open the report from the Operations → Reports menu.
  2. Set the date range / filters.
  3. Read on screen, or download/print the PDF where offered.
