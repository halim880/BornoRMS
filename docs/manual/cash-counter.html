Cash Counter

/operations/cash-counter

Who can access: CashierManager AdminSuperAdmin

Purpose

Reconcile physical cash in the drawer against the cash recorded by transactions for a shift/day.

How to reconcile

  1. Open /operations/cash-counter.
  2. Count the physical cash and enter the amount.
  3. Compare against the system's recorded cash total.
  4. Review the difference (over/short) and save the count.

Screenshot

Tip. Pair this with Accounts → Cash Accounts and the Cash Book report for a full daily cash trail.
