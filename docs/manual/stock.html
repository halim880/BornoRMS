Stock (Raw Materials)

/stock/items · /stock/goods-receipts · /stock/movements · /stock/wastage · /stock/low-stock · /stock/suppliers

Who can access: ManagerAdminSuperAdmin

Purpose

Track raw ingredients: on-hand quantity, average cost, deliveries, usage, and waste. This is the POS-side inventory (separate from Store).

Common tasks

Add stock items

  1. Open /stock/items → New; set name, unit, reorder threshold.

Receive a delivery

  1. Open /stock/goods-receipts; record supplier, items, quantities, cost. On-hand and average cost update.

Record wastage

  1. Open /stock/wastage; log spoilage/loss — it deducts from on-hand.

Watch low stock

  1. Open /stock/low-stock to see items below their reorder threshold; reorder from suppliers.
