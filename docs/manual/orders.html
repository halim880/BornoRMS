Orders

/orders · /waiter/orders

Who can access: SuperAdminAdmin ManagerWaiter ChefCashier

Purpose

Central list of all orders — placed by customers (web/QR) and by waiters. Track each order through its lifecycle and print receipts.

How to manage an order

  1. Open /orders. New orders appear in real time at the top.
  2. Click an order to open its detail (lines, quantities, table, notes, customer).
  3. Advance status: Pending → Preparing → Ready → Completed (or Cancel).
  4. Click Receipt to download/print the order receipt PDF.

Take a waiter order

  1. Go to /waiter/orders and choose the table.
  2. Add menu items, pick variants and quantities, add kitchen notes.
  3. Place the order — it routes to the Kitchen Display and Orders list.

Screenshot

Tip. Order numbers follow ORD-yyyyMMdd-NNNN (date-based). Status updates are live — no refresh needed.
