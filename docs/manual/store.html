Store (Finished Goods)

/store/items · /store/categories · /store/goods-receipts · /store/issues · /store/adjustments · /store/suppliers

Who can access: ManagerAdminSuperAdmin

Purpose

A separate warehouse for finished/prepared goods, fully isolated from POS Stock. Receive goods in, issue them to service points, and reconcile.

Common tasks

Set up items

  1. Create categories at /store/categories, then items at /store/items.

Receive goods

  1. Open /store/goods-receipts; record incoming finished goods (raises on-hand).

Issue goods

  1. Open /store/issues; issue items to a service point (lowers on-hand).

Adjust

  1. Open /store/adjustments to reconcile counted vs recorded quantities.

Screenshot

Two stock systems are separate by design — Store ≠ Stock. Do not expect numbers to merge across them.
