Menu & Modules (Navigation)

/admin/menu-permissions · /admin/modules · /admin/module-permissions

Who can access: AdminSuperAdmin

Purpose

The sidebar is DB-driven. These pages control which navigation modules and menu items each role can see.

Modules

  1. Open /admin/modules to CRUD top-level nav groups (e.g. Operations, Inventory, Accounts).

Menu permissions

  1. Open /admin/menu-permissions.
  2. For each role, grant or revoke visibility of menu items.
  3. Save — affected users see the updated sidebar on next load.

Module permissions

  1. Open /admin/module-permissions to control root module visibility per role.

Screenshot

Tip. If a staff member can't see a page they should, check their role here first — it's a navigation permission, not an account problem.
