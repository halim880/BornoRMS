Menu & Tables

/inventory/categories · /inventory/products · /inventory/tables · /operations/menu

Who can access: ManagerAdminSuperAdmin

Purpose

Define what you sell and where customers sit: menu categories, products (with prices and variants), and dining tables with their QR codes for self-ordering.

Product categories

  1. Open /inventory/categories.
  2. Add/edit a category and set its display order.

Products (menu items)

  1. Open /inventory/products → New.
  2. Set name, category, price, and variants/options.
  3. Toggle availability to show/hide on the customer menu.

Tables & QR codes

  1. Open /inventory/tables and add tables with numbers.
  2. Generate each table's QR code (deep-links the customer site to /menu?table=<id>).
  3. Bulk-print QR codes for placement on tables.
