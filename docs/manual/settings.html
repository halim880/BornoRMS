Settings

/settings/app

Who can access: All staff

Purpose

Personalize the console appearance.

Theme

  1. Open /settings/app.
  2. Pick a primary color in the ThemePicker — the UI updates live.
  3. Your choice persists (stored in the browser) for next time.

Screenshot

Tip. Theme is per-browser (localStorage), not a server account setting — it won't follow you to another device.
